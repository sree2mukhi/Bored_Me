---First Indian Women Teacher Savitribai Phule---




Savitribai Phule was an Indian social reformer, educationalist, and poet from Maharashtra. She is regarded as the first female teacher of India. Along with her husband, Jyothirao Phule, she played an important role in improving women's rights in India during the British rule.

She was born on January 3rd, 1831 in the village of Naigaon in Satara District, Maharashtra. At the age of 10, Savitribai Phule was married to Jyotirao Phule who was then a 13 year old boy.

Savitri was not educated at the time of her marriage, as she was a women and from a backward class. Her husband Jyotirao was forced to leave school because of his caste, but then he joined a Scottish missionary and studied till class 7

Savitri was educated at home by her husband and later on she took a teachers training course at an Institute run by an American missionary in Ahmednagar and in Pune.She then started teaching girls in Pune's Maharwada.

The Phule couple soon started their own school for girls and included western cirriculum and improved teaching methods. By the end of 1851 they were running 3 different schools for girls in Pune.In fact their methods were so good that the number of girls receiving education at the Phule's schools outnumbered the number of boys at government schools.

Savitribai and Jyotirao's efforts were met with constant resistance and disapproval from the conservative upper class community.It is said that Savitribai often carried an extra sari to school because she would be assailed by her conservative opposition with stones, dung and verbal abuse along the way. People labled them and their work evil, they were sent out on the streets by Jyotirao's father who didn't approve of their work.

Despite facing such extreme critisim Savitribai stood for her cause with a brave face and never wavered. She is an inspiration to many and is an iconic woman who fought for the rights of Indian women who were extremly oppressed during her time. She died a hero's death trying to save a plauge infected kid by carrying him to the hospital and in the process she was infected by the plauge as well.
